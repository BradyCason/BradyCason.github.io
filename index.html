About Brady Cason

As a Computer Science and Engineering student at UCI, I'm on a thrilling journey at the crossroads of technology, innovation, and teamwork.

I have an extensive background in Python, spanning several years. Additionally, I possess practical experience in a spectrum of languages, including C++, C#, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. My passion for mathematics and physics is the driving force behind my analytical approach to problem-solving, while my love for digital design allows me to infuse creativity into technical projects. This diverse skill set reflects not only my technical proficiency but also my commitment to continuous learning and innovation. Looking ahead, my goal is to thrive in the field of software engineering while sustaining a profound understanding of the foundational aspects of computer hardware.

In my school engineering projects, I've had the opportunity to delve into hands-on challenges that push the boundaries of innovation. During the first quarter, I took the lead in creating a remote-controlled rover, overseeing the design and fabrication of its chassis, steering system, and gear train. This experience sharpened my practical engineering skills and fostered a deep understanding of mechanical systems. In the second quarter, as a team captain, we embarked on the development of a fitness tracker using an Arduino and C++. This multifaceted project involved designing a system that tracked step count and flights climbed, displaying the data on an OLED screen and seamlessly integrating with a dedicated app. These projects not only enriched my technical expertise but also underscored the importance of effective collaboration and leadership in achieving project success.

I welcome connections with fellow students, professionals, and enthusiasts who share a passion for the sciences, mathematics, digital design, and technology. Whether you're interested in discussing the latest advancements or exploring collaborative projects that bring together technology and teamwork, let's connect and build bridges between diverse passions.
